About Us

Pinoy Plates is a website that highlights the varied cuisine of the Philippines. From the spicy Bicol Express to the sweet Halo-Halo, Pinoy Plates will take you on a gastronomic adventure across the archipelago. You will learn about the history, culture, and ingredients of each dish, as well as how to prepare them at home. Whether you are a Filipino looking for a taste of home, or a foreigner curious about the flavors of the Philippines, Pinoy Plates is your ultimate guide to Filipino food.

We are a team with a goal to inform people about the different cuisines in the Philippines, etc.
