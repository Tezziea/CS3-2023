Pinoy Plates

Welcome to Pinoy Plates, a website dedicated to showcasing the rich and diverse cuisine of the Philippines. Here you will find recipes, ingredients, and cultural insights into one of the most delicious and underrated cuisines in the world.

The Philippines is an archipelagic country in Southeast Asia, composed of more than 7,000 islands. The country has a rich history and culture, influenced by various Asian, European, and American civilizations. The cuisine of the Philippines reflects this diversity, with dishes that are savory, sweet, spicy, sour, and everything in between.
