Bleach

"Bleach" is a popular Japanese anime and manga series created by Tite Kubo. The story is set in a world where souls of the dead exist and can interact with the living. The protagonist, Ichigo Kurosaki, is a teenager with the ability to see the ghosts. One day, he accidentally acquires the powers of a Soul Reaper, a celestial being responsible for guiding souls to the afterlife and defending against evil spirits known as Hollows.

  • Shunsui Kyōraku
  • Shinji Hirako
